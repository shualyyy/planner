Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-01-01

Planer is a personal task and habit planner. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What we collect

  • Account data: your email address, and — if you provide them — display name and avatar color.
  • Content you create: tasks, habits, projects, comments, and habit completion history.
  • Collaboration data: Planer IDs you share, project members you invite, and pending invites.
  • Technical data: minimal server logs for reliability and debugging (IP address, browser/version).

How we use it

We use your data only to make the app work for you: syncing your content across devices, showing it to your teammates in shared projects, and diagnosing issues. We do not sell your data or share it with advertisers.

Where it's stored

All data is stored with our infrastructure provider, Supabase (Postgres + object storage), on servers in the region we've configured for the deployment. Access is protected by Row-Level Security policies scoped to your user account.

AI assistant

When you use the in-app assistant, the message you send and a summary of your tasks are transmitted to our LLM provider for the sole purpose of generating a reply. We don't retain your messages beyond the conversation on your device.

Analytics & tracking

We don't use third-party ad networks or behavioral trackers. If crash reporting is enabled in your build, only anonymized stack traces are sent to help fix bugs.

Your rights

You can edit or delete any of your content at any time from within the app. To request full account deletion or a copy of your data, contact us at the address below. We aim to respond within 30 days.

Contact:

Email: privacy@planer.app

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we'll update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify you inside the app.
